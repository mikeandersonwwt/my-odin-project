Taco Pizza

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Refried beans
  • Pizza dough

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle.
  3. Spread refried beans on the pizza dough.
  4. Spread ground beef on top of the refried beans.
  5. Top with remaining ingredients.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
